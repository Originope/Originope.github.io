      Chaos;Gate

        Crossover short story featuring Steins;Gate's protagonist Rintaro Okabe, takes place during Chapter 7.

      Delusion of Zero

        Can be read either as soon as you unlock the chapter Blue Sky, or immediately after finishing it.

      After finishing the true ending:

      Chaos;Head Love Chu Chu! (untranslated)

        Direct sequel to Chaos;Head NoAH. Chances of receiving an official English localization are low.


    Buy on Steam | Download improvement patch | Guide


      Steins;Gate Elite (improvement patch coming soon)

        Updated re-release of Steins;Gate that uses footage from the anime adaptation instead of traditional character sprites and illustrations.
        Only recommended for replays or those who wish to read Steins;Gate: Linear Bounded Phenogram. First time readers are strongly advised to choose the original version over Elite.

      After finishing the true ending:

      Holy Day of the Calamitous Birth

        Acts as an official, direct sequel to the Steins;Gate visual novel.

      Egoistic Poriomania

        Bonus OVA episode that takes place after Steins;Gate's events, in the anime adaptation's own separate canon.

      Load Region of Deja Vu

        Takes place after Egoistic Poriomania's events, in the anime adaptation's own separate canon. Watch immediately after finishing the OVA.

      A Posteriori Existence

        Acts as a prequel to Load Region of Deja Vu, should be listened to immediately after finishing the movie.

      Steins;Gate: My Darling's Embrace (improvement patch coming soon | Guide)

        Lighthearted romcom-themed spinoff set on the Delta attractor field.

      Steins;Gate: Linear Bounded Phenogram (improvement patch coming soon)

        Spinoff visual novel consisting of 11 short stories written by various guest writers, most of each focusing on a different character from Steins;Gate's cast.
        Is best saved for after Robotics;Notes Elite, as 2 of the stories contain references to Robotics;Notes that could potentially be considered spoilers.
        Cannot be purchased standalone. Only available as part of a bundle alongside Steins;Gate Elite.

      The Distant Valhalla

        Spinoff novella that takes place on the Alpha attractor field.

      Braunian Motion of Love and Hate

        Prequel manga that focuses on Yugo "Mr. Braun" Tennouji's backstory.

      Hyde of the Dark Dimension

        Drama CD that takes place on the Gamma attractor field, focusing on Okabe's relationship with Moeka on that worldline.

      Babel of the Grieved Maze (partially translated)

        Drama CD with manga and light novel adaptations that focuses on Kurisu during the events of Chapter 10: Paradox Meltdown.

      Amity of Budding Reminiscence (untranslated)

        Short story detailing how Okabe and Daru first met and became friends.

      Winter Sky Rocket (untranslated)

        Listen immediately after finishing Phase 7: Ionosphere Driver.

      The Unpublished Memoirs of Senomiya Misaki

        Prequel light novel and manga that explores Misaki Senomiya's backstory. Read as soon as you reach Phase 12: Heliosphere Connector.

      After finishing the true ending:

      Dream Seeker (untranslated)

      Maps, the Rain, and Macarons

        Short story focusing on Toshiyuki Sawada that takes place during Phase 9: Exosphere Breaker.

      Tomorrow in the Box

        An interlinked crossover story featuring the protagonists of all 3 previous mainline games. Can be read at any time once Chaos;Head NoAH,
        Steins;Gate and Robotics;Notes Elite have all been finished.

      The Memoirs of a Certain Wrong-Sider (untranslated)

      Slumbering Fools

        Drama CD that explores Mio Kunosato's backstory.

      Children's Collapse (partially translated, ongoing)

        Prequel manga series that also further explores Mio Kunosato's backstory, as well as her relationship with Steins;Gate's Kurisu Makise.

      Children's Collapse: First Time Going Out

        Short story set between chapters 3 and 4 of Children's Collapse.

      Chaos;Child Love Chu Chu!! (untranslated)

        Direct sequel to Chaos;Child. Highly unlikely to ever receive an official English localization due to NSFW content.

      Children's Revive (poor translation)

        Light novel set some time after the visual novel that acts as an ultimate, concluding epilogue for Chaos;Child.
        The current (and only) English translation available is readable, but considered to be of poor quality.

      Anime adaptation

        The Steins;Gate 0 anime acts more as a sequel to, or something of an "extended true ending route DLC" for the visual novel rather than a straightforward adaptation.
        Because of this, the anime is considered to be mandatory viewing after reaching the Milky Way Crossing ending in the visual novel.
